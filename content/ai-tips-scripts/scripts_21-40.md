## SCRIPT #21
**CATEGORY:** Systems & Operations
**AVATAR:** Stuck Intermediate / Forgotten Middle
**ON-SCREEN TITLE:** Use AI to take CRM notes — not skip them — to never lose a lead to a bad memory
**AI TOOL FEATURED:** Claude (voice-to-text)

**HOOK:**
Your CRM is only as good as the notes you actually put in it. And most agents put in almost nothing.

**PROBLEM:**
After every call or showing, agents tell themselves they'll update the CRM later. Later never comes. Six months go by, and your database is full of half-completed records with no context. When you finally reach back out to someone, you have no idea what you talked about, what they were looking for, or why they went cold. You're starting from zero every time.

**AI FIX:**
Right after any call or showing, open your voice-to-text app and spend sixty seconds talking — what the person said, what they care about, where they are in the process. Paste that voice memo into Claude and say: "Reformat this into clean CRM notes with a summary, next action, and follow-up date." Paste the result straight into your CRM. Done in under two minutes.

**REFRAME:**
Your database is your retirement account. Treat every note like a deposit.

**CTA:**
How up-to-date are your CRM notes right now? Scale of one to ten — drop it below.
