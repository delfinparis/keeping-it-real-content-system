**ON-SCREEN TITLE:** Use AI to build an expired listing outreach system — not cold-start every one — to convert more sellers who got burned

**HOOK:**

**PROBLEM:**

Ask Claude to also write a follow-up call script that opens with empathy, not a pitch. Personalized outreach to expireds closes at a dramatically higher rate than generic.

**REFRAME:**
Expired sellers don't need another agent who's confident. They need one who's thoughtful enough to ask what went wrong.

**CTA:**
Do you work expired listings? Tell me your current approach in the comments.
